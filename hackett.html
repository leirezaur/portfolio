Talent Clue

Hackett is a sophisticated fashion brand for menswear that stays authentic with the iconical British style in every piece they create.

After more than twenty years dressing men with high quality clothes, their experience and name is widely known.

An international common space to attract new talent to their stores and offices and unify their job applications was needed.

The page needed to show the spirit of the brand, their values and way of working, and the job openings posted regularly.

The brand already has a powerful visual image that suits the values of Hackett. The fonts used in their website are clear, British and elegant, with a modern feel. The main brand color is the Navy blue, combined with different scales of gray to emphasize its impact.

The previous Career site had general information about both Retail and Offices opportunities with very long paragraphs.

The layout was not attractive and users went directly to the positions part. They had to click one by one each country in order to see the open roles, which was not efficient and fast.

Working on the information architecture included thinking solutions to improve the navigation for candidates wanting to apply for stores or offices. A deep study of wireframes was carried out to reach the best way to arrange the content in a user-friendly way.

The Career Site is now divided into three landing pages that combine the brand’s photographies with its style and sophistication.
