about me

Leire by birth, @leirezaur online. I'm the one behind the fingerprint.

Hi there! I’m Leire, Lei or Leiris too. Techie, traveler, foodie and human. I’ve used too much internet, video games and apps in my life that my usability background is quite wide - well, my master’s degree also helps, I guess… I love sexy UI and my brain collapses everytime I deal with bad UX. Design runs in my blood and anything from architecture to 3D printing, analog photography or Pixar characters blows my mind and makes me more passionate about this huge field. My milestones in life so far include shaving my hair and traveling solo through Asia as a volunteer for 4 months. I also travel, take pictures and write in my personal travel blog and run a humble IG account (no millennial selfies, I promise!).

My main purpose in life is to make the world a more sustainable, user-friendly and human place to live in and to travel the whole 197 countries of the world - which one’s harder, huh?

what I do

When it comes to work, team mates would describe me as fast learner, adaptable and organized. Here you can see some of my professional skills and interests:

UI design

From user flows to wireframing or Design Systems. My experience as both graphic and UI designer makes me able to develop visually attractive ideas and high-fidelity and pixel-perfect deliverables.

UX design

I'm empathetic and try to listen to users to craft the best solutions to their needs. I'm familiar with Research tools in order to analyse them and collect the data to improve a product or experience.

Illustrations & icons

Basic in branding terms. I enjoy thinking and creating new assets following the guidelines of a brand. My good hand for drawing came out useful in my career for some needs in projects.

Interactions

Motion is essential in user experience. I'm interested in improving the way a product acts: from transitions between screens to actions and events behind the pure screen the user sees.

Team player

I'm not alone. Working in cross-functional teams has made me an adaptable human being that communicates with others, accepts feedback and understands other areas and points of view.

Agile

Iterations and design sprints are my daily routine. I still have a lot to learn about this methodologies but I see them essential when working in products, new features and teams.
