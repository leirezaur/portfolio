about me

Hello world! I'm Leire, a UX/UI designer based in Barcelona, Spain. I design, take care of small details. I think. I draw, I imagine. I write, travel and take pictures.

resume

What I do

I design. I think and overthink. I love drawing ideas and taking notes. And I love action. Some of my skills are listed below:

UI design

From user flows to wireframing or user interface. My experience as both graphic and UI designer gaves me a broad perspective to develop visually attractive ideas.

UX design

Human and user centered. I'm empathetic and try to listen to users to craft the best solutions to their needs. I'm familiar with Research tools in order to analyse them and collect the data to improve a product.

Team player

I'm not a loner. Working in multi-disciplinar teams has made me an adaptable human being that communicates with others, has curiosities and tries to understand other points of view.

Agile

Iterations and design sprints are in my daily agenda. I see them as an essential part of building a product and working in its improvements through time and user's interactions.
